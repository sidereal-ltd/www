boon2

boon2 is our in-house localisation technology built upon ultra-wideband technology. Our fusion of sensors and software achieves industry standard setting accuracy.

With this technology, robots, items, and people can be tracked to within centimetre accuracy, always.

Demonstration

Our UWB localisation system, boon2, can be used such that our robot can find its path, items are tracked during delivery and people are tracked using wearables.

Our unique in-house research and development allows us to achieve unprecedented localisation accuracy whilst keeping the costs down. This allows us to undercut the competition by a substantial margin for the same specifications.

Our system was designed from the grounds up to be scalable and to easily adapt to various use cases.
